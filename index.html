About Our Diocese

Serving Southern Malawi Since 1970

"We believe in holistic development that uplifts the most vulnerable in our communities."

Rt. Rev. Bishop Alfred Chaima, Diocese of Zomba

As the development arm of Zomba Catholic Diocese, CADECOM implements programs across Zomba, Machinga, and Mangochi districts. We work with local communities to address food insecurity, climate change adaptation, and social justice through Catholic Social Teaching principles.
