Our Identity

Serving Communities Since 1970

"We are called to walk with the most vulnerable, transforming communities through faith and development."

Rt. Rev. Bishop Alfred Chaima, Diocese of Zomba

CADECOM Zomba Diocese is the development arm of the Catholic Church in Southern Malawi, implementing programs across Zomba, Machinga, and Mangochi districts. We work to address root causes of poverty through:

  • Food security and sustainable agriculture
  • Disaster risk reduction and response
  • Women and youth empowerment
  • Advocacy for social justice

Our Mission

Rooted in Catholic Social Teaching

To empower vulnerable communities in Zomba Diocese through integrated development programs that promote human dignity, social justice, and sustainable livelihoods, inspired by Gospel values and the principles of Catholic Social Teaching.

Our Vision

A Transformed Society

A self-reliant society where all people in Zomba Diocese live in dignity, with equal opportunities to thrive spiritually, socially, and economically, in harmony with God's creation.
