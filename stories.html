Lives Changed in Zomba Diocese

Ellen Mponda
Farmer, Machinga District

From Hunger to Harvest Abundance

"Before CADECOM's irrigation program, my family struggled with hunger for 4 months each year. After receiving training and access to the drip irrigation system, I now grow vegetables year-round. Last season I earned MWK 450,000 from tomato sales - enough to pay school fees for all three children and build a better house."

Program Impact:
Ellen is among 78 farmers in TA Mwambo benefiting from the Climate-Smart Agriculture Project (2021-2023)
